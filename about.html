My semi-relevant life story

I started creating websites for fun back in the 90's. During that time I was finishing my degree in Physics and exploring my passion for writing and producing music. Eventually I found myself working as a financial analyst for some of the largest firms including Deutsche Bank, Bear Stearns and eventually JP Morgan. I was born and raised in New York and recently moved from Hoboken, NJ to Chicago, IL. I currently live in the western suburbs of Chicago with my wife and my 20-month-old son. I enjoy following Nasa's Planet Quest Program, reading the most recent research on Telomeres, playing the keyboard and spending time with my family.
